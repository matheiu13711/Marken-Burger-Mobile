Our Company

Marken Burger was founded by Matheiu Perdido in 2010 that offers customers pre-made combo meals, or the user can create their own. The objective is to offer high-quality burgers at a lower cost and the goal is to compete using ghost or cloud kitchens and conducting all operations online.
